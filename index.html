ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Macario Laurel, but you can call me Marco. All my life I've been passionate about the entertainment industry, most especially the video game industry. I have been eager to learn more about that industry and how games are made, which led me to pursue a career in game development. I have a background in game development, with knowledge in Unreal Engine 5, Unity, and Godot. My programming skills include C, C++, C#, Python, SQL, and Blueprints (Visual Scripting). I'm proficient with tools like GitHub, Visual Studio, and MySQL, and a bit of Tracktion Waveform for audio. I'm always eager to take on exciting projects that push the boundaries of interactive entertainment.
